Project Beacon

 

Client: Safesite, Inc.
Role: Senior Product Designer - Design Systems
Tools: Figma, Notion, Miro, UserTesting, Amplitude, FullStory, Storybook
Deliverables: UX Strategy, Design System, Prototype, GTM/Rollout Strategy

 

Safesite provides a workplace safety score to lower workers comp insurance rates. On October 2021, I joined a lean but ambitious team to help shape the ‘FICO’ score of safety.

 

Test

My Role

I joined the Safesite product team with the goal to improve and scale one of their most innovative products. Working closely with the SVP of product, I was responsible for the experience strategy and design of the Beacon Design system.

I lead the UX work, user research, producing all major deliverables and presenting those to the team. While I was the sole designer on this project, I worked alongside a team of product managers who focused on producing and validating features for the current and new platform.

 

The Challenge

A New Approach to Safety

From the start of the project, we were clear on two primary objectives — to optimize the app’s functionality and usability to meet and surpass their competitors; and to shift the conversation from safety being an after thought too being part of everyones daily workflow.
