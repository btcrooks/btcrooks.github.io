WOOO!

 

Company: ChatCook inc.
Role: Branding

Wooo logotype.

 

About

Wooo! is a paid time-based video and photo contest platform. The app enables users to participate in a variety of daily contests to win gift cards.

Challenge

Wooo! approached us with an existing lockup which they wished to refresh to feel more modern, friendly and fun.

Wooo's existing lockup

 

Discovery

Starting with a pen and pencil, I experimented with several concepts ranging from traditional text lockups to abstract shapes.

Wooo logo sketches

 

After carefull consideration, I began searching for a typeface that fit WOOO’s brand image.

Wooo typeface explorations

 

Solution

No typeface is perfect out of the box. We, tweaked, pushed and pulled pixels and ultimately reached a logotype that embodies the fun, spontaneous spirit Wooo! delivers to it’s users.
