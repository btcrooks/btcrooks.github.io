About Flip

Why make dating complicated? I wanted to infuse the most modern ways of dating into one simple and elegant app. My friends would always say, wouldn't it be amazing if you could filter people out, and only be shown people of your personal preference? I also chose to add a feature that has never previously existed, which will be revealed once the app launches in July.

The rules of the game are simple:

You select which ethnicities and height range you prefer. You then get matched with people who prefer your characteristics. Which makes it much more likely that you'll find someone you like. You can find people that are nearby within 250m radius and you can also flip through people that are as far as 150 miles away.
