LegalMatch Redesign

Brief: LegalMatch connects ordinary people with lawyers.

Approach: Given the nature of the site, I took a utilitarian over a stylistic approach by keeping many of the important links and info in view and directly accessible to the user, with plenty of call to actions.

I also felt it was necessary to remove the clunky 'table' of links from the home page. I feel it would be a better expereince to have a stand-alone page, or possibly an overlay, dedicated to showing frequently searched lawyers as well as search.
